## Quality Checklist

Before presenting the final plan, verify:
- Each day has a clear theme and practical route.
- Morning, afternoon, evening, and meals are not overloaded.
- Travel time and geography are plausible.
- Arrival and departure days account for luggage, check-in, and airport/station buffers.
- Seasonal constraints, closure days, reservation needs, and ticket risks are called out.
- Current facts are sourced where they materially affect the plan.
- Backup options fit the user's style instead of being generic filler.
- The plan clearly separates confirmed choices from assumptions.
